Fueled By Ramen

A Brief Introduction on Fueled By Ramen

Fueled by Ramen is an American record label, which is currently owned by Warner Music Group. The label was founded in Gainesville, Florida in the year 1996 by John Janick. Janick thought of starting a record label while he attended high school. He started the label while he was attending the University of Florida. He teamed up with Vinne Florello, the drummer from the band Less Than Jake, to make his record label dreams a reality.

The name of the band was inspirted by Florello and Janick's experinece in only affording cheap meals such as ramen during hte beginning of the growth of the record label. Both Florello and Janick invested the majority of their money into the label, which left them will little to no money for affordable food. Although, the label was founded in Florida, it is now based out of New York.The New York office doors were opened in 2007. The same year that Paramore'sRiot!album was released. The label prides its self for being more than just a label. They consider themselves a brand than a record label. They are a brand that embraces the community of punk inspired and rock/pop bands. To many fans of FBR bands, FBR is a lifestyle.

Paramore

Paramore is pop-punk band turned pop from Franklin, Tennessee. The band formed in 2004. It currently has two memebers, Hayley Williams and Taylor York. Williams is the lead vocalist and York is the band's guitarist. Due to conflict, three of the original memebers decided to leave the band. Paramore has all four albums recorded by Fueled by Ramen.

Panic! At the Disco

Panic! At the Disco is a pop band from Las Vegas Nevada. The band was formed in 2004. It currently has one of it's original members, Brendon Urie. The band split up because of different artistic ideas. All five albums have been recorded by Fueled by Ramen. Panic's recent album Death of A Bachelor is FBR's fastest selling album in the label's history. It sould 196,201 copies in it's first week.
