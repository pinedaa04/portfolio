Switchfoot
Neck Deep
Real Friends
With Confidence


Switchfoot is a alternative rock back from San Diego, California. The band was formed in 1995. They gained mainstream recognition from the movie A Walk to Remember. Four of their songs were included in the film and it's soundtrack.
Neck Deep is a pop punk band from Welsh. The band was formed in 2012. They are signed by Hopeless Records.
Real Friends is a pop punk band from Tinley Park, Illinois. The band was formed in 2010. They are signed by Fearless Records.
With Confidence is a pop punk band from Sydney, Australia. The band was formed in 2012. They are currently signed by Hopeless Records just like Neck Deep.

Rooney
DNCE
Melanie Martinez
Tor Miller


Rooneyis an rock band from Los Angeles, CA. The band was formed in 1999 by high school friends. It is currently the music project ofr frontman Robert Schwartzman.
DNCE is a funk pop band from Los, Angeles California. It was formed in 2015 by past boy band singer Joe Jonas.
Melanie Martinez is a singer songwriter from New York. Her music debut happend when she was a contestant on The Voice.
Tor Miller is a singer songwriter from New York. He is currently signed by GlassNote Records. He started off his career by singing at piano lounges around New York.
